🇬🇧 英语改写（300±20 字）

On July 14, with the Paris Olympics opening ceremony just a week away, French authorities have significantly escalated(升级) security measures. Massive demonstrations(示威游行) are expected, and police have deployed riot control(防暴) units to prevent any discontent(不满) from boiling over. The animosity(敌意) between protesters and forces highlights the ongoing tensions in the city.
